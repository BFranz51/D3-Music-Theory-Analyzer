INSTRUCTIONS:

There's an ocean of weird modes (scales) out there and this app serves as a lighthouse, illuminating good ones for you to use.

Simply select some notes you want to use. They can be all the notes of a melody or a chord progression. For best results, do this as soon as you start writing a new piece, and before you decide on every note. After you click the "Run Analysis" button, the app will crunch the numbers and display a graph showing suggestions.

NOTES:

  • Click "Change input notes" to bring up the piano so you can select notes
  • Select the root by clicking the yellow circles on the piano
  • Click "Change algorithm settings" to change how the app determines similarity between modes
